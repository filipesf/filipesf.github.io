My name is Filipe Fernandes and am a dad, husband, front-end developer & founder at Choco la Design. I started working as a designer in 2003 and as front-end in 2005 as a freelancer.

I provided consultancy to government agencies in Brazil like the Ministry of Health and Supreme Court about front-end architecture and UI design.

In 2010 I founded Choco la Design. It's a website where my contributors and I talk about design, helping our readers understand more about theory, technique and the market itself sharing our experiences.
